<h1>HamsterBase Tasks</h1>
<p>HamsterBase Tasks is a powerful, privacy-focused task management application that puts you in control of your
    data. Built with a local-first approach, it ensures your tasks are always accessible, even without an internet
    connection.</p>

<h2>Key Features:</h2>
<ul class="feature-list">
    <li>Instant Access: No waiting for server responses - all your data is stored locally on your device for
        immediate access.</li>
    <li>Seamless Multi-Device Sync: Access your tasks from any device - your phone, tablet, or laptop. Your work
        follows you everywhere.</li>
    <li>Offline-First Design: Work without internet? No problem! All data is stored locally and syncs automatically
        when you're back online.</li>
    <li>Long-Term Data Preservation: Your data stays accessible indefinitely, using durable formats and running on
        your local devices.</li>
    <li>End-to-End Encryption: Your data remains private and secure. Even the developers cannot access your
        decrypted information.</li>
    <li>Complete Data Control: You own your data. Store it on your device, create backups freely, and modify it
        without restrictions.</li>
    <li>Open Source: Built with transparency and community involvement. The codebase is open for inspection and
        improvement.</li>
</ul>
